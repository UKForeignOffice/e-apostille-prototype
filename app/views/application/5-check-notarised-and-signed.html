e-Apostille suitability - 3 of 3

Confirm documents are correctly prepared

To use the e-Apostille service, your documents must be:

  • prepared as PDF files
  • certified and electronically signed by a UK notary or solicitor

There is also guidance legal professionals must follow.

If you are submitting more than one document

Prepare each document that requires its own e-Apostille certificate as a separate PDF file.

If you include multiple documents in the same PDF file you will get a single e-Apostille that applies to, and must be shared together with, all of the documents in that PDF.

There is a fee for each e-Apostille issued.
