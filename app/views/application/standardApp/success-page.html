How long it takes and when you'll be charged

Applications take up to two working days, plus courier or postage time.

We manage payment as follows:

  • your payment card is charged immediately
  • if we haven’t received your documents within 28 days, your payment will be refunded and the money returned to your account - you will need to make another application
