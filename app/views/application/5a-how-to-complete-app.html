Completing your application

Based on your answers your documents are suitable for the e-Apostille service.

Next steps

  1. Step 1 Sign in or create an account

    You need an account to use the e-Apostille service. This is so that you can view the progress of your application and access your documents once legalised.

  2. Step 2 Upload your documents

    Upload the documents you want to be legalised in PDF files. We can issue one e-Apostille per PDF file.

  3. Step 3 Pay the application fees

    Each PDF costs Â£30. Pay by credit or debit card.

    If we cannot legalise any of your documents you will get an automatic refund of the fee for that file.

  4. Step 4 Download your legalised documents

    We will email you when your application has been processed. You will have 21 days to download PDFs containing your legalised documents and e-Apostille certificate.

    You can then digitally share your files with the overseas authority (for example, by email).
