Which service would you like?


Use this service to get an electronic Apostille (e-Apostille).

You need to:

  • make sure all PDFs have been certified with a single notarial cover sheet and digitally signed by a notary
  • upload one or multiple documents as a PDF (no bigger than 100MB), up to a maximum of 20 PDFs per application
  • pay £30 for each PDF
  • download the PDFs from your account within 21 days of them being available. Due to coronavirus (COVID-19) it may take 10 working days to legalise your documents

Some countries may not accept an electronic Apostille as proof that your document has been legalised.

{{ govukInsetText({ text: "Before you use this service, you need to check if an electronic Apostille will be accepted in the country you wish to use it. We cannot refund you if the Apostille is not accepted." }) }}

The standard service involves applying online and posting the documents you wish to legalise.

  • you need to post the documents you wish to legalise to the Legalisation Office
  • your legalised documents and Apostille will be returned to your chosen address by courier or post
  • due to coronavirus (COVID-19) it currently takes up to 10 working days to process your documents, plus courier or postage time
  • it costs £30 per document, plus courier fees or postage
