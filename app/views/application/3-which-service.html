Which service would you like?


This is a new service to issue an electronic apostille (eApostille).

Check before you use this service if the apostille will be accepted in the country you wish to use it. We cannot issue a refund if the apostille is not accepted.

About the eApostille service
  • the documents you wish to legalise must be certified and digitally signed by a notary
  • all documents must be uploaded as a PDF file (no bigger than 50MB per file)
  • if you need to legalise more than one document at the same time, you can upload them as a bundle by putting all documents in a single PDF
  • your legalised documents including an eApostille will be issued and made available to download from your account
  • due to coronavirus (COVID-19) it currently takes up to 10 working days for an electronic apostille to be available to download from your account
  • it costs £30 per eApostille issued

The standard service involves applying online and posting the documents you wish to legalise.

About the standard service
  • you need to post the documents you wish to legalise to the Legalisation Office
  • your legalised documents and apostille will be returned to your chosen address by courier or post
  • due to coronavirus (COVID-19) it currently takes up to 10 working days to process your documents, plus courier or postage time
  • it costs £30 per document, plus courier fees or postage
