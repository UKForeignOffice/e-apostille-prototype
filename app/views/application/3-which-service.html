Use this service to get the documents legalised with an electronic apostille (e-Apostille).

You can:

  • upload documents you want to legalise, for example a passport, as a PDF
  • upload multiple documents in a single PDF (for example a passport and a driving licence)
  • pay £30 for each uploaded PDF, even if it contains multiple documents
  • upload up to 50 PDFs in a single application
  • avoid courier or postage costs

You need to:

  • check if an e-Apostille will be accepted in the country in which you want to use it (no refund if an e-Apostille is not accepted)
  • ensure all PDFs are notarised and digitally signed by a notary
  • download the PDFs (including e-Apostilles) from your account within 21 days of the documents being legalised

The standard service involves applying online and posting the documents you wish to legalise.

  • you need to post the documents you wish to legalise to the Legalisation Office
  • your legalised documents and Apostille will be returned to your chosen address by courier or post
  • it costs £30 per document, plus courier fees or postage
