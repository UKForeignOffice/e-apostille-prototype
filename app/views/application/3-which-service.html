Which service would you like?


Use this service to get the documents legalised with an electronic apostille (e-Apostille).

How the e-Apostille service works:

  • you need to upload the documents you wish to legalise as a PDF, for example a passport
  • you can upload more than one document, for example a passport and a driving licence, as a single PDF
  • all PDFs must be notarised and digitally signed by a notary
  • there is a maximum limit of 20 PDFs per application
  • it costs £30 per uploaded PDF
  • once the documents have been legalised you need to download the PDFs from your account within 21 days.
  • due to coronavirus (COVID-19) it may take up to 10 working days to legalise your documents

The standard service involves applying online and posting the documents you wish to legalise.

  • you need to post the documents you wish to legalise to the Legalisation Office
  • your legalised documents and Apostille will be returned to your chosen address by courier or post
  • due to coronavirus (COVID-19) it currently takes up to 10 working days to process your documents, plus courier or postage time
  • it costs £30 per document, plus courier fees or postage
