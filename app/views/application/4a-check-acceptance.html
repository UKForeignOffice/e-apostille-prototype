Check if the documents are eligible for the e-Apostille service

Most types of documents are eligible for the e-Apostille service if they have been notarised and digitally signed by a notary.

However, some documents are not eligible for the e-Apostille service. You cannot use the e-Apostille service if the document you wish to legalise is:

  • issued by the General Register Office (GRO), for example, birth, death, marriage, civil partnership and adoption certificates
  • ACRO police certificate for England and Wales
  • Disclosure Barring Service (DBS) certificate for England and Wales
  • disclosure certificate for Scotland
  • disclosure certificate for Northern Ireland
  • Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) membership certificate
  • fingerprint certificate
