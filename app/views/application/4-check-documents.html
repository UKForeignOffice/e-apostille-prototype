Check the authority accepts e-Apostilles

Ask the overseas authority exactly what they need from you and if they will:

  • accept digital documents with e-Apostilles
  • require separate e-Apostille certificates for each document (if legalising more than one)
  • accept documents signed by a UK notary or solicitor, or either

Make sure they understand e-Apostilles are only valid in digital form as they contain unique electronic signatures.
