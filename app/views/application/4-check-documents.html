Check if your documents are eligible for the eApostille service

We accept most types of documents if they have been certified by a notary.

You cannot use this eApostille service if your document is:

  • issued by the General Register Office (GRO)
  • ACRO police certificate for England and Wales
  • Disclosure Barring Service (DBS) certificate for England and Wales
  • disclosure certificate for Scotland
  • disclosure certificate for Northern Ireland
  • Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) membership certificate
  • fingerprint certificate
  • certified by the British Council
